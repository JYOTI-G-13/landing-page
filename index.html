How to Make a Delicious Dish

Step-by-step guide to making a perfect meal at home.

Ingredients You'll Need

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional spices: turmeric, cumin

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add onions and garlic, saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add rice to the pan and stir for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Pour in 4 cups of water, add salt and pepper.
  5. Cover the pan and let it simmer until the rice is fully cooked.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
